/ I love coding and designing websites /


Hi, my name is Alros Avellana


I'm an aspiring Web-Developer. I discovered my passion for coding around December 2016. It was an instant obsession. Now I'm hoping to turn it into my career.


I pride myself in my ability to write plain vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript without any plugins or frameworks. I believe that new frameworks can be learned but a good foundation is a must. For now my focus is Front-end Development. I just love the Design and Creative aspect of it but I will learn back-end development soon enough!


I started my learning through freeCodeCamp and other online resources where I logged over 400+ hours of coding and learning front-end development including 150 hours of algorithm challenges, and 6 projects that range from basic to advanced. The varying difficulty and requirements of each project unquestionably helped me grasp and understand what I needed to learn. That doesn't mean my learning is over, in-fact its just begun!

Calculator

See the Pen JavaScript Calculator App by Alros Avellana ( @alrosavellana) on CodePen.

Here I created a fully functioning calculator with jQuery and Bootstrap without any API being used. The challenge for me here was not so much the calculating part but rather placing limitations on what the user can do and see such as not being able to press any operator and have it show up on the screen multipe times or before any number is pressed. Its the multiple minute things like that which I could only see through user testing that makes it feel like a real working calculator.
